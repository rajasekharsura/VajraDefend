How It Works

Scoped to Secured
in 4 Steps

A transparent, structured engagement process. You know exactly what's happening at every stage — no surprises on timeline, scope, or cost.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We map your full attack surface — web apps, APIs, infrastructure, and third-party integrations. We agree on objectives, rules of engagement, and timeline before anything begins.

02

Attack Simulation

Engineers execute real-world attack scenarios using current TTPs from the MITRE ATT&CK framework. Always manual testing — not just automated scanning and report generation.

03

Report & Debrief

CVSS-scored findings with proof-of-concept screenshots, business impact analysis, and prioritised remediation steps. Personal debrief call with your technical team included.

04

Remediate & Retest

We work alongside your team during remediation, answer every technical question, and verify each fix through a complimentary retest — confirming nothing was left open.
